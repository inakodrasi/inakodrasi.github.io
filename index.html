Dr. Ina Kodrasi

Postdoctoral Researcher
Idiap Research Institute, Martigny, Switzerland

Ina Kodrasi received the Master of Science degree in Communications, Systems and Electronics in 2010 from Jacobs University Bremen, Germany and the PhD degree in 2015 from the University of Oldenburg, Germany. From 2010 to 2015 she worked as a researcher at the Signal Processing Group of the University of Oldenburg. From 2010 to 2011 she was also with the Fraunhofer Institute for Digital Media Technology (IDMT), Project group Hearing, Speech and Audio Technology in Oldenburg where she worked on microphone array beamforming. From 2015 to 2017 she was a postdoctoral researcher at the Signal Processing Group of the University of Oldenburg in the field of speech dereverberation and noise reduction. Since December 2017 she is a postdoctoral researcher at the Speech and Audio Processing Group of the Idiap Research Institute, Martigny, Switzerland working in the field of signal processing for clinical applications.
